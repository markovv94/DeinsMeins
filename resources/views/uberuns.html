Our Story

More than just a cafe.

Deins&Meins was born from a simple idea: why choose between good food and good design when you can have both?

Founded in 2023, we created a space where the aroma of freshly roasted coffee blends seamlessly with the visual delight of handcrafted decor. We believe in the power of "Regionalität" — supporting our local community through both our kitchen ingredients and the artisans whose work graces our shelves.
